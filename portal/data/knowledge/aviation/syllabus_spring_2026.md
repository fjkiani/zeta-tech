
# Aviation High School - Spring 2026
## Course: AMT 101 - Introduction to Airframe & Powerplant

### Mission Statement
To prepare students for FAA certification and careers in aerospace maintenance through rigorous academic and technical training.

### Curriculum Schedule

#### Week 1-3: Safety & Regulations
- **Topic**: Shop Safety, OSHA Standards, and FAA Regulations.
- **Reference**: FAR Part 43 (Maintenance, Preventive Maintenance, Rebuilding, and Alteration).
- **Activity**: Tool Control Procedures and Foreign Object Debris (FOD) walks.

#### Week 4: Hydraulic Systems Fundamentals
- **Topic**: Fluid Dynamics and Power Systems.
- **Key Concepts**: 
  - Pascal's Law (Force = Pressure x Area).
  - Hydraulic Fluids: Mineral base (Red) vs Phosphate Ester base (Skydrol/Purple).
  - Seals, Packings, and O-rings identification.
- **Lab Work**: Disassembly of a basic hydraulic actuator.

#### Week 5: Electrical Systems
- **Topic**: Basic Electricity and Electronics.
- **Key Concepts**: Ohm's Law, Series & Parallel Circuits, Schematic Reading.
- **Lab Work**: Multimeter usage and circuit board troubleshooting.

#### Week 6-8: Sheet Metal Structures
- **Topic**: Metallic Aircraft Structures.
- **Skills**: Riveting (Universal & Countersunk), Bend Allowance calculations, drilling.
- **Project**: Fabricate a control surface patch repair.

### Required Materials
- TI-30XIIS Calculator
- Safety Glasses (Z87+ rated)
- A&P Technician General Textbook (Jeppesen)
