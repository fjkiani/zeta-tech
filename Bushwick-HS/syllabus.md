## üìú THE "SPEC-FIRST" RULE
*Before you touch the keyboard, you write the Spec. This IS your prompt.*

1.  **GOAL**: What does this tool DO?
2.  **INPUTS**: What does the user type?
3.  **OUTPUTS**: What does the screen show?
4.  **EDGE CASES**: What if the user types "banana" instead of a number?

## üõ†Ô∏è THE TOOL BOX (Weekly Arcs)

### WEEK 1: THE QUIZ BOT (Variables & Conditionals)
- **Goal**: A bot that asks questions and tracks score.
- **Micro-Tools**:
    - *Day 1 (Greeter)*: `input()` + `print()`.
    - *Day 2 (Judge)*: `if/else` (Right/Wrong).
    - *Day 3 (Looper)*: `while` (Keep asking until right).
    - *Captsone*: "The Impossible Quiz".

### WEEK 2: THE HOMEWORK PLANNER (Lists & Loops)
- **Goal**: A CLI tool to manage your life.
- **Tech**: Lists to store tasks. Loops to print them.
- **AI Mode**: "Reviewer Mode" (Find my bug).

### WEEK 3: THE TEXT CLEANER (String Manipulation)
- **Goal**: Paste messy text, get clean text.
- **Tech**: Methods (`.strip()`, `.upper()`).
- **AI Mode**: "Tutor Mode" (Explain this function).

### WEEK 4: THE GRADE CALCULATOR (Functions & Math)
- **Goal**: "What do I need on the final to pass?"
- **Tech**: Custom Functions. Return values.
- **AI Mode**: "Test Writer" (Generate 5 edge cases to break my code).
